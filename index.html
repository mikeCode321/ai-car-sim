How to Use the Self Driving Car Simulation

1.a Controls: Use the 'Save' button to save the car that goes furthest in the simulation.

1.b Controls: Use the 'Discard' button to discard the current state.

2.a Mutation Rate: The closer to 0, the more similar the weights are to the 'best' car. Press 'Enter' to save input.

2.b Mutation Rate: The further from 0, the more sporadic the mutations are for the weights of the off-spring in relation to the 'best' car. Press 'Enter' to save input..

3 Simulation: Refresh the page and repeat until at least one car makes it past all obstacles.

Happy Tinkering!
